---
title: Temporary Flight Restrictions (TFRs)
description: Temporary Flight Restrictions (TFRs) are airspace restrictions that are put in place to protect people and property on the ground or in the air.
---

## What is a TFR?

## Types of TFRs

A TFR can be issued for a variety of reasons, including:
- [14 CFR § 91.137](https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-14/section-91.137), TFRs in the vicinity of disaster/hazard areas
- [14 CFR § 91.138](https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-14/section-91.138), TFRs in natural disaster areas in the State of Hawaii
- [14 CFR § 91.141](https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-14/section-91.141), TFRs in the proximity of the Presidential and other parties
- [14 CFR § 91.143](https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-14/section-91.143), TFRs in the proximity of space flight operations
- [14 CFR § 91.144](https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-14/section-91.144), TFRs
